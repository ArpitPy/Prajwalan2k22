INNOV-A-THON

The hackathon aims to bring together the best minds in technology to develop innovative solutions to real-world problems.
1. Participants will be able to showcase their ideas and compete for prizes in front of a panel of judges.
2. The hackathon is open to all individuals with interest in technology, regardless of their experience level.
3. The event is a platform for participants to learn new skills, collaborate with others, and potentially turn their ideas into successful startups.

Rules

  1. Every team has to register online on the Prajwalan’22 Techfest website for the competition. A Team ID will be allocated to the team on registration which shall be used for future references.
  2. A team can register at any point of time before 15 th February 2023.
  3. Each team has to carry all the necessary equipment as per their need, which will be required for the HACK-A-THON. (Example laptops, any devices, sensors, electronics components, etc…)
  4. The decision of the organizers and/or judges shall be treated as final and binding on all. Prajwalan’22 Techfest has all the rights to verify the identity and accuracy of the details provided by the participants.
  5. Prajwalan’22 Techfest will hold no responsibility for any late, lost, or misdirected entries.
  6. The idea presented by the teams should be original (not protected by patent/copyright/technical publication by anyone else).
  7. Note that at any point in time the latest information will be that which is on the website.
  8. All modes of official communication will be through the it.prajwalan22@gmail.com e-mail ID.
