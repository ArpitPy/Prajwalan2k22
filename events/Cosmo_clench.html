Cosmo Clench

The aim of COSMO CLENCH is to find best minds for industry who are able to learn and apply their robotic knowledge in front of others.

1. Participants will have the opportunity to showcase their innovative bot which is able to travel over the entire areena and compete with other team bots for prizes in front of a panel.

2. The COSMO CLENCH is open to all individuals with an interest in bot development, regardless of their experience level.
